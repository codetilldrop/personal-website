Notable Experience

Experience is the best way to learn. Some of the best experiences I have had include:

  • Attending UNSW to complete the HS1511 computer science course. The course was designed to challenge high school students in a university environment and allow them to finish their first semester of CS and obtain univeristy credit. The course was extremely challenging in many ways. There were many technical tasks to complete which hurt my brain and I had to balance it out with school and social life. With hard work and thirst for knowledge, I managed to complete the course and gained a whole lot of experience about how to prioritise and manage time as well lots of technical knowledge.
  • Attending NCSS Summer Camp 2019. NCSS is a 10 day intensive course held at University of Sydney. During the camp, I was placed in an embedded devices stream where we gathered an introductory understanding to electrical engineering and computer science. We programmed microbits to do various tasks and the final goal was to make a game with microbits. Our team made a game of robotic laser tag which turned out quite successful. Despite the sleep deprivation, this camp was one of the most invaluable experiences I have ever had.

Projects

Ideas. I love ideas and furthermore, turning them into products. Some of the coolest projects I have worked on include:

  • Building a programming language and compiler for a conceputal microprocessor. Throughout this project, I learnt so much about the fundamentals of a computer and how programming languages work. I learnt about how to define syntax for a language, building parse trees, recursive decent parsing and just how complex the nature of what a seemingly simple language might be.
  • Making a social media site. Through this project, I gathered a knowledgeable understanding of databases, APIs and UX design
  • Game Development. I have built many mini games which demonstrate basic game mechanics and UX development knowledge. A few have been published on the internet, with my favourite being 'Ziggy'. https://dillondesilva.github.io/Ziggy/
  • Making an online school planner. This project was very similar to the social media site, however, it was one of the first projects where I realised that good thoughts and ideas can be used to build beneficial tools. For me, this was an important step to becoming a better engineer.
  • Prefix Trie NPM Package. Building a module to create and interact with prefix tries was super awesome as well as getting to publish it as an npm package.
  • Smart lighting. I built a smart lighting system for my room using some neopixels as well as microbits. One of the coolest projects I've done
  • Making a garden automation system for my mother so she can check the moisture levels of her plants from her phone to work out if they require watering
  • Building an iOS app called Russ. Russ is a free to download iOS app/game which aims to educate people about Climate Change

Currently, I am focusing on writing machine learning programs as well as working with a variety of robotics for my side projects.
